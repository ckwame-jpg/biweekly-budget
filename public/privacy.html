Privacy Policy

Bi-Weekly Budget · Last updated 18 July 2026

Bi-Weekly Budget (“the app”) is a personal budgeting tool. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app collects, why, and the choices you have. We collect as little as possible and we do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

What we collect

  • Your email address — only if you choose to create an account for cloud sync. It identifies your account and lets you sign in on more than one device.
  • Your budget data — the income, expense, savings, and debt figures you type in, plus your saved pay-period history. This is the content of the app.

That’s it. The app has no ads, no third-party analytics, no tracking pixels, and does not collect your location, contacts, or device identifiers for advertising.

Where your data lives

  • On your device. The app works fully offline; your budget is stored locally in your browser/app storage whether or not you ever create an account.
  • In the cloud, only if you sign in. If you create an account, your budget is also saved to our database provider, Supabase, so it can sync across your devices. Each account’s data is isolated by row-level security — only your signed-in account can read or write your rows.
  • The website itself is hosted on Vercel.

How we use it

Your email is used to authenticate you and to send account emails (such as a password reset link when you request one). Your budget data is used only to provide the app’s features to you. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to anyone.

Your choices & rights

  • Export your data at any time from Settings → Backup → Export JSON.
  • Delete your account at any time from Settings → Cloud sync → Delete my account permanently. This erases your account and your cloud data, and clears the copy on your device. It cannot be undone.
  • Use the app without an account — simply don’t sign in, and no data ever leaves your device.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we’ll update the date above and, where appropriate, note it in the app.
